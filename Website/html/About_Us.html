Our History



Zumba was created by the choreographer and fitness instructor Alberto “Beto” Perez in the mid-1990s.

After forgetting his music for aerobics class, he improvised using the salsa and merengue tapes he

had on him. Instead of strict rep-counting, the improvised class was the perfect mix of dance and

aerobics that kept the class fun.

Zumba was designed to be low-impact to suit people of all ages and from different fitness levels, but

it is also an intense and effective calorie-burning workout. Zumba’s philosophy is to help anyone reach

their fitness goals in a supportive environment while having as much fun as possible.
