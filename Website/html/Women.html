Our Instructor


Karina was first introduced to Zumba when she

was on a cruise with her parents at age 11, and has

had the bug since! As a long time member of the

Zumba Instructor Network (ZIM), Karina knows the

ropes and is confident teaching people from all walks

of life. Karina will make you feel welcome and can

adapt her class to suit any fitness level.
